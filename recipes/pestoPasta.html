Pesto Pasta Recipe


Photo by Dotdash Meredith Food Studios">

Description

This pesto pasta recipe is a simple and delicious dish that can be prepared in just 20 minutes. It's perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or a light lunch.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz pasta (spaghetti, penne, or your choice)
  • 1/2 cup basil pesto (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: fresh basil leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the pasta and cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain the pasta, reserving about 1/4 cup of the pasta water.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked pasta with the basil pesto. If the sauce is too thick, add a little of the reserved pasta water to loosen it up.
  3. Add the grated Parmesan cheese and cherry tomatoes to the pasta mixture. Toss everything together until well combined.
  4. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper to taste. Toss again to ensure everything is evenly coated.
  5. Serve the pesto pasta in bowls, garnished with fresh basil leaves if desired. Enjoy your quick and tasty pesto pasta!
